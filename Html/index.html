What is Asean?

ASEAN stands for the Association of Southeast Asian Nations, a regional intergovernmental organization comprising ten countries in Southeast Asia.





ASEAN's Diversity

ASEAN brings together countries with significant differences. ASEAN`s geography includes archipelagos and continental land masses with low plains and mountainous terrain.
