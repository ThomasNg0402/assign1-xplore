Do you recognise all the Asean members? Hover over the flags below and discover a fun fact about the country!

BRUNEI

"Uncover the tranquil beauty of Brunei, where pristine rainforests meet stunning mosques. Immerse in rich culture, enjoy vibrant markets, and explore the Sultanate's serene landscapes."

CAMBODIA

"Step into the ancient wonders of Cambodia. From the awe-inspiring Angkor Wat to bustling markets, discover a land steeped in history, warmth, and timeless charm."

INDONESIA

"Dive into Indonesia's diverse archipelago. Picture lush landscapes, vibrant ceremonies, and pristine beaches. Experience a tapestry of traditions, flavors, and natural wonders."

LAOS

"Find tranquility in Laos, where lush mountains cradle ancient temples. Explore the Mekong River, savor local cuisine, and discover the genuine warmth of its people"

MALAYSIA

"Experience Malaysia's cultural mosaic. Indulge in diverse cuisines, venture into vibrant cities, and explore tropical rainforests, where modernity seamlessly blends with nature."

MYANMAR

Unearth Myanmar's hidden treasures. From ancient temples in Bagan to serene lakes, encounter a country brimming with spirituality, traditions, and genuine hospitality.

PHILIPPINES

"Revel in the Philippines' tropical paradise. Sink your toes into pristine beaches, dive into vibrant coral reefs, and embrace a warm, hospitable culture."

SINGAPORE

"Discover the dynamic city-state of Singapore. Skyline views, cultural festivals, and delectable cuisine await, offering a harmonious blend of modernity and tradition."

THAILAND

"Embark on a Thai adventure, where bustling markets meet ancient temples. Indulge in savory street food, relax on idyllic beaches, and absorb the Land of Smiles' vibrant energy."

VIETNAM

"Journey through Vietnam's rich tapestry. From the bustling streets of Hanoi to the serene beauty of Ha Long Bay, experience a nation steeped in history, charm, and breathtaking landscapes."
